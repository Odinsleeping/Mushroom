Definition of a mushroom!

A mushroom or toadstool is the fleshy, spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus, typically produced above ground, on soil, or on its food source. Toadstool generally denotes one poisonous to humans.

Places to find MushroomüçÑ

Mushrooms require the right amount of light and the right temperatures. Most mushrooms prefer shade or dark places, which is why you'll often find them on forest floors.
